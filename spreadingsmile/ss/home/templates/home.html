Mission

Our mission is to distribute clothes and such usable items which are no longer use of people at their home. At times of pandemic, the consequences have been bad for poor people economically. So providing them winter clothes, shoes can help them a lot. We are not asking for money but only the things which are not useful for you. Our volunteer will pick the things from your home or you can also join us to distribute whenever their is distribution drive and we will send the venue and time. You just have to provide the information what you can donate and when. You can also contact us through website and mail if you have any queries.
